About Me

My name is Kevin. I am a UI/UX Designer.

I'm an UX Designer with a background in sustainability and software engineering. I like solving complex problems to create intuitive experiences. My journey into design was unconventional. I took the traditional path to obtain a degree in ecosystem science and sustainability. I worked as a sushi chef to support myself through school, and during that time, I discovered a passion for photography. When I found UX Design, everything finally clicked into place. It was the perfect blend of my interests: psychology, human interaction, problem solving, creativity, and technical skills.
